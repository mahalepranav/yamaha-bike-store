Scooter 125 Voluntary Recall

     Dear Valued Yamaha Customer,

     This information is in accordance with the requirements of our parent company Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd., Japan, who
     have determined that a defect, which relates to Brake lever function may exist in few Fascino 125 & RayZR 125 scooters
     within certain production numbers range. Hence this defect must be repaired at the earliest in these scooters.

     For your convenience, Yamaha is initiating a Voluntary Recall Campaign, where the affected scooters must get defected
     parts replaced with a modified one.

     We at Yamaha are committed to provide all necessary support to ensure that your scooter always gives you its optimum
     performance.

     Please note that the replacement of these parts shall be free of cost.
